How-does-this-work?: This is a template to illsutrate how a BrainHack project is presented

by People that are curious, motivated and friendly

This project aims at bringing together students and scientists aiming at understanding the brain.
How cognition is articulated in the brain is one of the most challenging research topic. When conduting research project a cross-disciplinary approach is often more likely to achieve significant progress.
The spirit of BrainHack is to bring together participants with different skills that will be helpful for a given research project. The share interest and motivation are the main ingredient to work within a collective intelligence perspective.

The following points are going to be addressed in this project:
  • Discussions about the milestones and presentation of the methods used
  • Modeling for sanity check
  • Visualization for data quality check

Required skills

This is a multimodal project, a mix of coding and non-coding skills are required:
Knowledge in cognitive neuroscience 100%
Motivation 100%
Brain imaging data processing 75%
Programming 50%
Data visualilzation 50%
